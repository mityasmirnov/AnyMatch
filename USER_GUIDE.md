## Introduction

**AnyMatch** is a Tinder-style web application that helps you and your friends find movies to watch together. Instead of endless scrolling and indecision, simply swipe through movies and TV shows. When everyone in your group likes the same title, it's a match!

### Key Features

- **Tinder-Style Swiping**: Swipe right to like, left to pass, up for super like, down to save for later
- **Group Matching**: Create groups with friends or partners and find movies everyone wants to watch
- **AI Recommendations**: Get personalized suggestions based on your taste and swipe patterns
- **Streaming Availability**: See where each movie is available to stream (Netflix, Disney+, etc.)
- **Beautiful UI**: Liquid glass design with smooth animations and responsive layout

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

AnyMatch uses Manus OAuth for secure authentication. No need to create a separate account!

**Steps to Sign Up:**

1. Navigate to the AnyMatch homepage
2. Click the **"Get Started"** button
3. You'll be redirected to the Manus login page
4. Sign in with your Manus account or create a new one
5. After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to the swipe interface

### First Time Setup

After logging in for the first time, we recommend:

1. **Set Your Preferences**: Navigate to the Profile page and select your favorite genres, preferred content type (movies, TV shows, or both), and minimum rating threshold
2. **Create or Join a Group**: Go to the Groups page to create your first group or join an existing one with a friend's join code
3. **Start Swiping**: Head to the Swipe page and start discovering movies!

## Managing Groups

Groups are the core of AnyMatch's social features. Create groups with friends, family, or your partner to find movies everyone wants to watch.

### Creating a Group

**Steps:**

1. Navigate to the **Groups** page from the navigation bar
2. Click **"Create Group"**
3. Enter a group name (e.g., "Friday Movie Night", "Family Watchlist")
4. Click **"Create Group"**
5. You'll receive a unique 6-character join code (e.g., "ABC123")
6. Share this code with friends so they can join

### Joining a Group

**Steps:**

1. Get the join code from the group owner
2. Navigate to the **Groups** page
3. Click **"Join Group"**
4. Enter the 6-character join code
5. Click **"Join Group"**
6. You're now part of the group!

### Viewing Your Groups

On the Groups page, you'll see cards for each group you're in, showing:

- Group name
- Your role (Owner or Member)
- Join code
- Number of members

### Copying Join Codes

To invite more people to a group:

1. Find the group card on the Groups page
2. Click **"Copy Join Code"**
3. Share the code via text, email, or any messaging app

### Viewing Group Matches

Click **"View Matches"** on any group card to see movies that everyone in the group has liked. This is your shared watchlist!

## Viewing Matches

The Matches page shows all movies that everyone in your group has swiped right on. These are the movies you should watch together!

### Match Grid

Matches are displayed in a grid with movie posters. Hover over a poster to see:

- Movie title
- Content type (Movie or TV)
- Rating

### Watched Status

After watching a movie, you can mark it as watched. Watched movies will have a green checkmark badge in the top-right corner.

### Group Members

At the bottom of the Matches page, you'll see all members of the group with their roles (Owner or Member).

## Saved Movies

The Saved Movies page shows all movies you've saved for later by swiping down.

### Filtering

Use the dropdown at the top to filter by content type:

- **All**: Show both movies and TV shows
- **Movies**: Show only movies
- **TV Shows**: Show only TV series

### Actions on Saved Movies

For each saved movie, you can:

- **Swipe Now**: Make a decision (like/dislike/super like)
- **Remove**: Delete from your saved list
- **View Details**: Open the movie details modal

### Making Decisions

When you're ready to decide on a saved movie:

1. Click **"Swipe Now"**
2. A swipe interface will appear
3. Swipe in any direction to make your choice
4. The movie will be removed from your saved list

Customize your movie discovery experience by setting your preferences.

Click **"Profile"** in the navigation bar to access your profile page.

Select your favorite movie genres to get more relevant recommendations:

- Drama
- Horror
- Romance
- Sci-Fi
- Thriller
- And many more!

**Content Type:**

Choose what type of content you want to see:

- **Movies**: Only movies

After updating your preferences, click **"Save Preferences"** at the bottom of the page. Your changes will be applied immediately to your swipe feed.

## AI Recommendations

AnyMatch uses AI to provide personalized movie recommendations based on your swipe history and preferences.

### How It Works

The AI recommendation system analyzes:

1. **Your Swipe Patterns**: Movies you've liked, disliked, and super-liked
2. **Genre Preferences**: Your favorite genres from your profile
3. **Collaborative Filtering**: What similar users are watching
4. **Content Attributes**: Directors, actors, themes, and plot elements

### Getting Recommendations

AI recommendations are automatically integrated into your swipe feed. The system continuously learns from your behavior and adjusts suggestions in real-time.

### Improving Recommendations

To get better recommendations:

- **Swipe Consistently**: The more you swipe, the better the AI understands your taste
- **Update Your Profile**: Keep your genre preferences current
- **Use Super Likes**: Super likes signal strong preferences to the AI
- **Explore Different Genres**: Occasionally swipe on movies outside your comfort zone to help the AI understand your range

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Movies Not Loading

**Problem**: The swipe interface shows a loading spinner indefinitely.

**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

#### Can't Join a Group

**Problem**: "Invalid join code" error when trying to join a group.

**Solutions**:
- Verify the join code is correct (6 characters, case-insensitive)
- Ask the group owner to confirm the code
- Make sure you're not already a member of the group

#### Swipes Not Registering

**Problem**: Swiping on cards doesn't seem to work.

**Solutions**:
- Make sure you're logged in
- Check if you're in a group or solo mode
- Try using the buttons instead of gestures
- Refresh the page

#### Matches Not Appearing

**Problem**: You and your group members liked the same movie, but it's not showing in matches.

**Solutions**:
- Verify all group members have swiped right on the movie
- Refresh the Matches page
- Check if you're viewing the correct group's matches

#### Streaming Providers Not Showing

**Problem**: Movie details modal doesn't show streaming availability.

**Solutions**:
- Some movies may not have streaming data available
- Streaming availability varies by region
- Check back later as data is updated regularly

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered in this guide:

1. Check the GitHub repository for known issues
2. Submit a bug report with detailed steps to reproduce

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is AnyMatch free to use?**

A: Yes, AnyMatch is completely free. You only need a Manus account to sign in.

**Q: How many people can be in a group?**

A: There's no limit! You can have as many members as you want in a group.

**Q: Can I be in multiple groups?**

A: Absolutely! You can create and join as many groups as you like.

**Q: What happens if I leave a group?**

A: Currently, you can't leave a group once joined. This feature is coming in a future update.

**Q: Can I see what movies my friends have swiped on?**

A: No, individual swipes are private. You'll only see matches when everyone in the group likes the same movie.

**Q: Does AnyMatch work on mobile?**

A: Yes! AnyMatch is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

**Q: Where does the movie data come from?**

A: AnyMatch uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API for all movie and TV show information.

**Q: Can I suggest features or report bugs?**

A: Yes! Visit the GitHub repository to submit feature requests and bug reports.
